I'm a career changer training to become a financial adviser. Before this, I studied philosophy, taught English in Thailand and Italy, worked in edtech, and spent a lot of time trying to figure out what I actually wanted to do.
When I started studying for R01, I found the revision process frustrating — dense material, limited practice questions, and no way to know whether what I'd studied last week had actually stuck. So I built the tool I wished existed.
CIIReady uses spaced repetition to schedule your revision around how memory actually works. I wrote every question myself, based on publicly available regulatory material, and I use the app daily for my own studies.
